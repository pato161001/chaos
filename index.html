Who We Are

Built For
The
Underground

Chaos Circuit is an event company rooted in techno culture. We curate and produce high-intensity electronic music events across Europe — from intimate warehouse raves to large-scale open-air festivals.

We work exclusively with artists and venues that share one principle: no compromise on sound. Every event is engineered from the ground up — lighting, acoustics, lineup, capacity. Nothing is accidental.
